     What is Freedom-

     
      The quality and state of being free, it is,
      1. The absence of necessity, coercion in choice or action. 
      2. Freedom from care. 

    so financial freedom means you have the ability to manage your money in such a way that you have sufficient fund to live your chosen lifestyle without being constrained or requiring assistance form others.

     

  • Elements of financial freedom-

    1. Sufficient time for yourself and freedom from cares.
    2. High level of peace and happiness in life 
    3. 5–10-year expensive cover or 5–10-year savings 
    4. Having an emergency fund, investment in stocks and other instruments and free from all debts. 
    5. high level of personal and professional relationships satisfaction.
    6. doing whatever you want and whenever you want.

    Step 02- income diversification--- 

  • Benjamin franklin said about financial freedom-

    • "There're two ways to increase your wealth increase your income or decrease your want, and the very best is do both at the same time increase your income and also decrease your want. 

    In the journey of financial freedom, the very next step after deciding to become financial free is your diversification of your income source and it will look as shows below- 

    1. Running bank account (60% of your income source) - This account for 

    a. Daily life expensive (food, cloths, bills, mote rage, etc.
    b.  Travel/holidays
    c. Other Buying stuffs  

    2. Saving bank account (10% of your income source)-

    Account for saving, in this account you can’t use for buying stuffs another thing.  this is an account for long-term saving so that you have an amount of money in long Term for sustain your lifestyle after your retirement so that you can maintain your lifestyle.

    3. Emergency bank account (10% of your bank account)- This account is for 

    • I. Financial emergency (debt payment and other)  
    • II. Medical emergency in case of health issues)
    • III. Other emergency (education payments etc.)
    • IV. Don’t use the account for any unnecessary stuff. 

    4. Investment account (20% of your income value) - an account for investment 

  • Use for investment in FD’S, Mutual funds, SIP’S, Stocks, bonds and the very best in index funds. 
  • If you have no ideas about investment, go to your bank and ask them about FD’S,   Mutual funds, SIP’S they will help you to do very best with low risk. 
  • If you want to know the very best you can go for internet for the very best knowledge. 
  • After some time, investment will become your second income source. 

    • Golden rule of investment- Always go for long term and use compound interest in your favor. 

    Have an example- 

    A. If you have 1,00,000 amount per month income, then 

    ---- Account fund in the end of year
    1 Running account 60,000 ---------------
    2 Saving account 10,000 1,20,000.00
    3Emergency account 10,000 1,20,000.00
    4 Investment account 20,000 2,40,000.00

    you can see that after a year you have good amount of money saved and good amount of money invested, also you generate another income source from your investments.
